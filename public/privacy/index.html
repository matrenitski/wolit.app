Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

wolit.app is a Bitcoin wallet that runs entirely in your browser. It has no backend server. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit your personal data or your Bitcoin keys to any server we control — there isn't one.

1. No servers, no data collection

wolit.app is a static web application served as plain files. There is no wolit.app server that receives, stores, or processes your information. Everything happens locally in your browser.

2. Signing in with Google

When you sign in with Google, your browser receives your basic profile (name, email address, and profile photo) from Google so the app can show which account you are using. This stays in your browser and is not sent to us. We request only the drive.appdata permission, which lets the app read and write a single hidden, app-specific folder in your own Google Drive. It cannot see, read, or touch your other Google Drive files.

3. Your Bitcoin key

Your wallet's private key (your 12-word recovery phrase) is generated in your browser and stored only in that hidden folder of your own Google Drive. It is never sent to wolit.app or to any third party. We have no access to it and cannot recover it.

4. Blockchain data

To show balances and send payments, your browser contacts a public Bitcoin data provider (mempool.space). Your Bitcoin address and signed transactions are sent there to look up balances and broadcast payments. This is how the Bitcoin network works; it does not include your name or Google identity. That provider has its own privacy terms.

5. Fonts

The app loads the Inter font from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of serving the font file.

6. Cookies & tracking

wolit.app uses no analytics and sets no tracking cookies. Google's sign-in may set cookies that are necessary for authentication.

7. Local storage on your device

The app may keep small, non-sensitive preferences in your browser (for example, which accounts have signed in before, so they can be shown to you on return). This never leaves your device.

8. Children

wolit.app is not directed at children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The date above reflects the latest version.
