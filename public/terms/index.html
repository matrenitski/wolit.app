1. What wolit.app is

wolit.app generates a Bitcoin wallet in your browser and stores its key in your own Google Drive. There is no operator who can access your funds. You — and only you, through your Google account — control the wallet.

2. Your responsibilities

  • Your Google account is the key to your money. Keep it secure with a strong password and two-factor authentication. Anyone who controls your Google account can access this wallet.
  • Back up your 12-word recovery phrase and keep it private and safe.
  • Check every transaction before sending. Bitcoin payments are irreversible — there is no "undo" and no chargeback.
