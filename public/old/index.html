Frequently Asked Questions

How does wolit.app keep my wallet secure?
Your private key is stored in a hidden, app-specific folder in your Google Drive (appDataFolder). This folder is invisible to you and can only be accessed by wolit.app. It syncs automatically across all your devices when you sign in with the same Google account. The wallet is protected by Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure.
What stablecoins are supported?
wolit.app supports major USD and EUR stablecoins on the Ethereum network: • Traditional stablecoins: USDT, USDC, DAI • MiCA-compliant European stablecoins: USDQ, EURQ, USDR, EURR All balances are fetched in real-time from the Ethereum blockchain.
Do I need to remember a seed phrase?
No! That's the beauty of wolit.app. Your wallet is tied to your Google account, so you don't need to remember or store any seed phrases. Simply sign in with Google from any device to access your wallet. If you want to backup your private key manually, you can view and copy it from the wallet dashboard.
Can I access my wallet from multiple devices?
Yes! Your wallet automatically syncs across all devices where you sign in with the same Google account. Whether you're on your phone, tablet, or computer, your stablecoins are always accessible. The private key is securely stored in your Google Drive's hidden application folder.
What happens if I delete my wallet?
If you delete your wallet using the "Delete Wallet" button in the dashboard, your private key will be permanently removed from Google Drive storage. This action cannot be undone, and any remaining funds in that wallet will become inaccessible forever. Always transfer your funds to another wallet before deleting. After deletion, you can create a new wallet from scratch.
Is wolit.app free to use?
Yes, wolit.app is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges. You only pay the standard Ethereum network gas fees when sending transactions, which go directly to the Ethereum network validators, not to wolit.app.
Can I use wolit.app with a business Google account?
Yes, wolit.app works with both personal and business Google accounts (Google Workspace/G Suite). We use a non-sensitive OAuth scope that doesn't require special permissions. However, if your organization has strict policies that block third-party Drive access, you may need to contact your IT administrator to allow wolit.app.
How do I send stablecoins?
Sending stablecoins is simple: 1. Click the "Send" button in your wallet dashboard 2. Select the stablecoin you want to send 3. Enter the recipient's Ethereum address 4. Enter the amount to send 5. Review and confirm the transaction The transaction will be broadcast to the Ethereum network and typically confirms within a few minutes.
Can I export my private key?
Yes, you can view and copy your private key from the wallet dashboard at any time. This allows you to import your wallet into other Ethereum wallet applications if needed. Remember to keep your private key secure and never share it with anyone, as it provides complete control over your wallet and funds.
